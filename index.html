This graph represents the relationship between total population and median earnings per state across the United States. The data was pulled from the U.S. Census Bureau and the Behavioral Risk Factor Surveillance System, and it focuses on the accessibility to Health Care throughout states. I have chosen Total Population and Median Earnings because there is a direct correlation to this research. By clicking on each individual bubble it will show you the data for each state.
